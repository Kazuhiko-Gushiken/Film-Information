# Film Information (WIP)

A general Film Information repository. Containing specific film developing instructions, information on developing, tips and tricks to film photography, custom inserts, as well as printing information.

### <u>I cannot stress this enough</u>: This repository is new and under development. It's become a long term project of mine to collect information about film photography (and about photography in general) through first hand experiences, watching YouTube, or raw research and compile it here as sort of a notebook as well as a place for others to learn. I cannot garuntee everything will be 100% accurate (please open an issue if you think something is wrong), I'm still somewhat new to film photography, only about a year of experience.
