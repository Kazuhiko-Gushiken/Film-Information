## Ilford HP5 Plus 400

This is a top choice film stock for its forgiving nature, making it ideal for various lighting conditions.

At box speed, you'll get full tonal range, good shadow detail, moderate contrast, and moderate grain. It's a safe and balanced choice for a wide range of situations.

You can [overexpose](/Techniques/Exposure%20vs%20Development.md) it by one [stop](/Terminology/Stop.md) to get better shadow detail, smoother tones, and slightly denser negatives.

Ilford HP5 Plus 400 uses a traditional cubic-grain silver halide emulsion.
