## Kentmere Pan 400

It's a cheaper stock, but it can be quite flat at box speeds, especially in low-contrast lighting conditions. Kentmere Pan 400 responds well to pushing, which can improve contrast and increase grain.

A two-stop push to ISO 1600 can produce significantly increased contrast and a more pronounced grain structure while still retaining usable shadow detail.

Kentmere Pan 400 uses a traditional cubic-grain silver halide emulsion.
